About Us

started in 2023 as a one-off idea, "shot by johan." soon became my way of documenting my life especially when i entered my final year of high school. currently with more than 50 vlogs, "shot by johan." is my longest running project (the only one i have been able to stick to properly).

room 319 was my hostel room in the first year of college and happened to be one of the cheapest domains i could possibly buy. it embodies my transition from abu dhabi to kerala and how life changed. today, this site exists merely as an archive of my previous creative work and possibly a portfolio of sorts.
